What is the hooke's law?
Hooke's law states that the strain of the material is proportional to the applied stress within the elastic limit of that material.
How to calculate hooke's law?
To calculate hooke's law you should use the following formula:
Fs = -k * x
where:
Fs = spring force
k = spring constant
x = spring stretch or compression
Why use hooke's law?
Hooke's law is used in order to ensure that the components can withstand a specific level of force that is calculated prevously.
